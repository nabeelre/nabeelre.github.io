Non-Parametric Spherical Jeans Mass Estimation with B-Splines

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor

Excerpt from abstract:
We present a new unbinned non-parametric routine for solving the spherical Jeans equation by fitting B-Splines to the 3-dimensional velocity and density distributions of halo stars obtained by the Gaia survey and spectroscopic surveys such as DESI. We validate our routine on several progressively more complex and realistic mock datasets.
draft of the journal paper

Star Formation Rate in Merger Galaxies

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor
MDM Observatory - Kitt Peak National Observatory

Excerpt from abstract:
We observe four target merger galaxies in H-alpha to calcualte their star formation rate densities and collect previously observed data giving us their gas surface density. With these two quantities, we can investigate how various merger galaxies stand in relation to non-mergers with respect to the Kennicutt-Schmidt law.

Other Projects

Website Architect for GHOSTS HST Survey

University of Michigan & Leibniz Institute for Astrophysics

I recently undertook a project rebuilding the Galaxy, Halos, Outer disks, Substructure, Thick disks and Star clusters (GHOSTS) HST survey website. I noted inefficiencies in the source of the current version and designed a rebuild to correct for these and improve expandability. I proposed my rebuild project to GHOSTS P.I. Dr. Roelof de Jong with logistical help from GHOSTS team member and UM professor, Eric Bell. My rebuild employs Jinja , an efficient Python templating engine, to simplify the creation and modification of their webpages.
